ABOUT US

Maison Lourmel is a French perfumery brand. Created by two brothers in love with quality perfumes, for perfume lovers. We know what you want, because we are like you. Of the irreproachable quality of the exceptional scents which will make you travel from the East to the West. All our creations are unisex and conceived in collaboration with great names in perfumery, located in Paris and Grasse.
